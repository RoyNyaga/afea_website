arts and craft


AFEA has been promoting and developing arts and crafts by making things with our own hand and mainly things one would consider waste. Recycling and waste transformation is the main drive of AFEA arts and crafts for healthy environment and economic empowerment. AFEA uses materials that are considered useless and waste to come up with useful and consumable products that beautifies your environment beyond expectation. The AFEA handicraft center gives it members an exposure to DIY (Do it Yourself) thereby empowering your creative minds which can enhance better life for many young and enthusiastic cameroonian who will become entrepreneur in this field. Crafts items are usually sold to economically empower and help in raising seed capital to the individuals. AFEA trains women and young people with no formal education into arts and crafts. These activity raises income and gives the financial independence. Visit Our Handicraft center to have a firsthand testimony of our DIY Products.
